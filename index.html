Privacy Policy for Hawrami Keyboard

Effective Date: [5-5-2025]

Data Collection

This app does not collect, store, or share any personal information.

Word Suggestions

The keyboard uses a built-in offline dictionary with over 100,000 Hawrami words. Suggestions are generated locally on your device, and no data is sent over the internet.

Child-Friendly

This app is safe for children and users of all ages. It contains no ads or in-app purchases.

Third-Party Services

No third-party services (analytics, ads, crash tracking) are used in this app.

Permissions

The app requires permission to function as your keyboard. This is essential for functionality, not data collection.

Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy. Check this page for the latest version.
